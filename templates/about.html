Welcome to ApniPaathshala!

Education has the power to transform our world, our lives, our community our families and more importantly our lives.

In ApniPaathshala we believe that, no matter who you are or where you are, everyone in the world is entitled to a free education of best quality. So here we are to provide a platform connecting teachers and students.

Teachers who are willing to give free education to a underprivileged students join our website and provide study material and different type of activity. This help a students to get all the study material at one place and have a focused study.
